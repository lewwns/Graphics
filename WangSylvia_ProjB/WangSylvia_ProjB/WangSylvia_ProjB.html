User Instructions:

Click Run|Stop to run or stop every rotated things on the screen.

Press the key W, key S, key A, and key D to change the four look up, down, strafe left and right.

Press the direction key to look forward, backward, left, and right.

Press the key Y to see the flying-airplane yaw.

Drag the mouse to rotate the apple.
